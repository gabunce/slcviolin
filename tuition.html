TUITION

Lessons are divided into three semesters: Spring, Summer & Fall.

Tuition includes private lessons, group lessons, and recital fees (excluding accompanists). The number of private lessons offered during a semester are divided by the number of months. There will be between three and five lessons a month due to holidays and breaks and one group lesson.

Payment is due the first lesson of every month. You can pay by semester or month.

    REGISTRATION FEE

    A one time $30 registration fee helps cover the cost of materials and items I will be sending home with you. Due at registration.

    SUZUKI ASSOCIATION OF UTAH FAMILY MEMBERSHIP

    Students are required to join the SAU for a $30 fee, once a year in September. You must sign up with a registered Suzuki teacher. Large recitals and masterclasses are possible through this wonderful organization. Students will benefit a great deal!

    ACCOMPANIST FEES

    Students who are beginning their musical journey in Book 1 will benefit from me accompanying them at lessons and recitals. But beyond these first pieces, students will need an accomplished pianist to play with them during formal recitals. In this case we will hire an accompanist as a studio and the cost will be split evenly among students.
